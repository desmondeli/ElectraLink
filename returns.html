Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

📦 Electra Link accepts returns within 7 days of delivery for eligible products in original, unused condition.

1. Eligibility for Return

A product is eligible for return if:

  • It was delivered damaged or defective
  • It is the wrong product (different from what you ordered)
  • It is returned within 7 days of the delivery date
  • It is in its original packaging with all accessories, manuals, and seal intact
  • It has not been used, activated, or tampered with

2. Non-Returnable Items

⚠️ The following items cannot be returned once delivered:
  • Products that have been activated or registered to a Samsung account
  • Products with broken seals or missing accessories
  • Products damaged by the customer after delivery
  • Chargers and accessories that have been used
  • Products returned after 7 days from delivery

3. How to Initiate a Return

1
Contact us within 7 days of delivery via WhatsApp (+233 59 797 3525) or email with your order number and reason for return.
2
Send clear photos or a short video showing the issue with the product.
3
Our team will review and approve your return request within 24–48 hours.
4
Ship or bring the product to us in its original packaging. Return shipping costs are covered by Electra Link for defective/wrong items.
5
Once received and inspected, your replacement or refund will be processed within 3–5 working days.

4. Refunds

Approved refunds are processed via the same payment method used for the original purchase (Mobile Money, bank transfer, etc.) within 3–5 working days of return approval.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us at Electra Link.

Last updated: May 2025

1. Who We Are

Electra Link is an authorised Samsung dealer based in Accra, Ghana. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: username, email address, and password (stored locally on your device)
  • Order information: your name, delivery address, phone number, and email when you place an order
  • Communication: any messages you send us via WhatsApp, email, or social media
  • Usage data: pages visited, products viewed, and cart activity stored locally in your browser

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To communicate with you about your orders and enquiries
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send you relevant promotions (only if you opt in)

4. Data Storage

This website stores your account details, cart, favourites, and order history in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any external server by the website itself. Order details shared during checkout may be processed by Electra Link internally.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Delivery partners, solely to fulfil your order
  • Samsung Ghana, for warranty claim purposes only
  • Payment processors, to complete your transaction

6. Your Rights

  • You can clear your local data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • You can request deletion of any order data we hold by contacting us
  • You can opt out of promotional messages at any time

Cookie Policy

How Electra Link uses browser storage to improve your experience.

Last updated: May 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device by your web browser. This website primarily uses localStorage — a similar browser-based storage mechanism — rather than traditional cookies. Both serve a similar purpose of remembering your preferences and session data.

2. What We Store and Why

Storage KeyWhat It StoresPurpose
ss_sessionYour login status and usernameKeeps you logged in across pages
ss_usersYour account credentials (locally)Enables login and signup
ss_cartItems in your shopping cartPersists cart between page visits
ss_favsYour favourite productsSaves your wishlist locally
ss_historyYour recent search termsShows recent searches on your profile

3. Third-Party Storage

This website does not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other external tracking at this time.

4. How to Clear Your Data

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by:

  • Going to your browser settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Local storage
  • Or opening browser Developer Tools → Application → Local Storage → clearing keys starting with ss_

Note that clearing this data will log you out and empty your cart and favourites.

Warranty Policy

All products sold at Electra Link carry genuine Samsung warranty.

✅ All products sold by Electra Link are 100% genuine Samsung products covered by Samsung's official manufacturer warranty.

1. Warranty Coverage

Samsung products purchased from Electra Link come with the standard Samsung Ghana manufacturer warranty:

  • Smartphones & Tablets: 12 months from date of purchase
  • Laptops & Desktops: 12 months from date of purchase
  • Wearables (Watches, Buds, Ring): 12 months from date of purchase
  • Chargers & Accessories: 6 months from date of purchase

2. What the Warranty Covers

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Hardware failures not caused by external damage
  • Screen defects not resulting from physical damage
  • Battery defects (significant capacity loss within warranty period)

3. What the Warranty Does NOT Cover

  • Physical damage — cracked screens, dents, liquid damage
  • Damage from unauthorised repair or modification
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality
  • Products with removed or altered serial numbers
  • Damage from power surges or improper charging

4. How to Make a Warranty Claim

To make a warranty claim, contact Electra Link with the following:

  • Your order number or proof of purchase
  • A description and photos of the defect
  • The product's serial number (found on box or device settings)

Reach us via WhatsApp at +233 59 797 3525 or email us. We will guide you through the Samsung service centre process.

5. Warranty Claim Timeline

Once a valid warranty claim is submitted and the product is assessed by an authorised Samsung service centre, the process typically takes 7–14 working days depending on parts availability.
